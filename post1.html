Bounty Cheesecake Recipie

Ingredients:

350g of coconut cookies 2 spoons of butter
500g of cream cheese 120g of coconut shrims
500g of 36% fat sour cream 100g of honey
half a tea spoon of salt 6 Bounty candy bars
Start with lining the cake mold with baking paper. Then melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat. Put coconut cookies into the blender. Mix the cookies and then add butter and work with the blender until you get the consistency of a wet sand.
Line the bottom of the cheesecake in a cake mold and press it neatly to the bottom. Put the mold in the fridge while making cheesecake mass.
In a large bowl, mix cream cheese with honey to a fluffy consistency. In a separate bowl whip the cream stiffly.
It's time to create a cheesecake mass - for this combine the contents of both bowls. You can use a blender for this, but only use it to combine the ingredients. At the very end, pour in the coconut shreds and mix again, this time by hand.
Take out the form with the bottom of the cake from the fridge and put half the cheesecake mass in it.
In the blender mix Bounty bars and form a flat mass, which gently put on the first layer of cheesecake.
Finish the cheesecake with the remaining cheesecake mass and level it with a spatula. Now you have to put the cheesecake in the fridge for at least 4 hours, although I recommend leaving it there all night.
Decorate with cocoa, white chocolate topping, and/or some more cut Bounty candy bars.
